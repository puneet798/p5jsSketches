About the work
This work is a search for a bird.
This work cannot be rendered till the cybernetic loop between machinic sense, screen, your mind and body, a bird in the sky, the many birds that have come before that have trained the machine-learning model and your network connection closes. When this loop closes, a text of a few charecters is generated.
We see this as a performace.
The time of the day, weather, the population of birds, the location of the machine/human, the 4 texts we have selected, the autors of these texts, their lives and the non humans that they have encountered, all these together make a story of which we imagine ourselves to be a part of. And now by reading this text, you too are inevitably part of this performance. *
It is to see this text that we make this work in performance with you. The generated text is a to the many poetics and performances that have come before us and have made us who we are.
It is to see this text that we make this work.
if it cannot see, it is not there. THis is a work of bodies. I cant social distance from my phone if you see the bird in the screen the device has an eye now it is seeing and it can tell that it is a bird. a projected consience
