Creation of Birds

  • Give access to the camera of your device. The camera would become analogous to the eye and start naming objects, human, and non-humans in the vicinity. Don't panic!
  • Search for a bird outside of your window through this eye. Keep the camera in focus and wait for it to say - "Bird Detected".
  • Once you, your camera, and the bird become one - a text will be generated on the screen.
  • Click a screenshot and share!
    • Works best on cell phones and tablets
